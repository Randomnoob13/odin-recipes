Sussy Stew

The perfect stew to begin your day!

By: RandomNoob13 | Difficulty: x x x

Gather the required ingredients:

  • Brown Mushroom
  • Red Mushroom
  • Bowl
Open the crafting table or inventory crafting grid. Place one brown mushroom, one red mushroom, and one bowl in the crafting grid in the following pattern:
B B B
R R
Bowl
B: Brown Mushroom R: Red Mushroom. Once you have arranged the ingredients correctly, Mushroom Stew will appear in the result box.

Enjoy your Minecraft stew!
