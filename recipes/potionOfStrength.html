Potion of Strength

By: RandomNoob13 | Difficulty: x x x

Gather the required ingredients:

  • Nether wart
  • Blaze powder
  • Ghast tear
  • Redstone
  • Spider eye

Requirements: Brewing stand, Glass bottles, Blaze powder

Place the brewing stand on the ground. Fill the glass bottles with water by right-clicking on a water source while holding the glass bottle. Place the filled glass bottles in the brewing stand. Add the Nether wart to the top slot of the brewing stand. This will create awkward potions. Add the blaze powder to the fuel slot of the brewing stand to activate it. Wait for the awkward potions to brew.

Enjoy your Potion:

Once the brewing process is complete, remove the awkward potions from the bottom slot of the brewing stand. Add the blaze powder again to activate the brewing stand. Add the ghast tear to the top slot of the brewing stand. Finally, add the spider eye to the top slot of the brewing stand to create the potion of strength.
